# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 16, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how mimiDLC Discord Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services. By using the Bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you interact with the Bot, we automatically collect certain information, including:

#### Discord User Information
- **User ID**: Your unique Discord user identifier
- **Username**: Your Discord username and display name
- **Avatar**: Your Discord profile picture URL (if publicly available)
- **Server Membership**: Information about which servers you share with the Bot

#### Command Usage Data
- **Command Name**: Which commands you use
- **Timestamp**: When you use commands
- **Server ID**: Which server the command was used in (if applicable)
- **Usage Frequency**: How often you use certain features

#### Interaction Data
- **Button Clicks**: When you interact with Bot-generated buttons
- **Message Content**: Content you provide when using specific commands (e.g., price alert criteria, todo items, story subscriptions)
- **Message Metadata**: Message timestamps and channel IDs for spam detection (content NOT stored)

### 1.2 Voluntarily Provided Information

You may voluntarily provide additional information when using certain Bot features:

- **Price Alerts**: Asset symbols and price thresholds you set
- **Todo Lists**: Task descriptions you create
- **Story Subscriptions**: Stories you subscribe to and subscription types
- **Story Forum**: Story threads you create and notification settings
- **User Preferences**: Settings and preferences you configure

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect or permanently store:
- Private or direct messages you send to other users
- Full message content (only metadata like timestamps for spam detection)
- Messages in servers where the Bot is not present
- Payment or financial information
- Real names, email addresses, or phone numbers (unless you explicitly provide them in a command)
- IP addresses or precise geolocation data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Service Provision
- To provide and maintain Bot functionality
- To process your commands and requests
- To deliver notifications and alerts you've requested
- To personalize your experience with the Bot

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- To analyze usage patterns and improve Bot features
- To identify and fix technical issues
- To develop new features and functionality
- To optimize performance and user experience

### 2.3 Safety and Security
- To detect and prevent abuse or misuse of the Bot
- To enforce our Terms of Service
- To protect the rights and safety of users
- To comply with legal obligations

### 2.4 Communication
- To respond to your support requests
- To send important service announcements
- To notify you of significant changes to the Bot or this Privacy Policy

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored in secure databases hosted on our servers. We use:
- **PostgreSQL**: For persistent data storage
- **Redis**: For temporary caching and session management

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encrypted database connections
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits and updates
- Secure server infrastructure

### 3.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services:
- **Command Usage Data**: Retained for analytical purposes, typically for 90 days
- **User Preferences**: Retained until you delete them or stop using the Bot
- **Price Alerts**: Retained until you delete them
- **Todo Items**: Retained until you delete them
- **Story Subscriptions**: Retained until you unsubscribe or the story thread is deleted
- **Spam Detection Metadata**: Retained temporarily in cache (typically hours to days)

You can request deletion of your personal data at any time (see Section 7).

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The Bot integrates with the following third-party services:

- **Discord**: We use Discord's API to provide Bot functionality. Discord's [Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy) applies to data they collect

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

## 7. Data Deletion

### 7.1 How to Request Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data by:
1. Contacting us through our official support channels
2. Providing your Discord User ID for verification
3. Specifying what data you want deleted

### 7.2 Deletion Process

Upon receiving a valid deletion request, we will:
- Verify your identity
- Delete the requested data within 30 days
- Provide confirmation of deletion
- Retain only what is legally required
